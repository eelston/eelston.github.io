KIM-1 LED Matrix Art

E&C-ENG 197DP | Spring 2023 | Python, 6502 assembly language

Components

  • KIM-1 Clone (from Corsham Technologies, no longer sold)
  • DotStar 8x8 LED Grid (from Adafruit)
  • Breadboard
  • Online assembly language to hex compiler (asm80.com)

Background

In Spring 2023, I took 197DP, an ECE course for hands-on experience which was described as follows:

  • Weekly hands-on electronics lab with term project. Weekly three-hour scheduled lab.
  • Additional two hours of studying/reading/prototyping expected outside of the scheduled lab time.
  • Electronic circuit schematic diagrams, circuit theory, LEDs, transistors, ICs (integrated circuits) and other electronic components.
  • Assembly language programming and microprocessor peripherals.
  • No prior experience with electronic circuits or computer programming required.


Through this course, I was introduced to assembly language and the KIM-1 clone, a board developed by Corsham Technologies replicating the MOS Technology 6502 single-board computer. WIP, come back soon!
